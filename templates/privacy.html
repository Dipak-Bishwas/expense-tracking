Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic account details such as your name, email address, and password. We also collect the expense records you choose to log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment card numbers, bank credentials, or any information beyond what you directly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Service to you — displaying your expense history, generating category breakdowns and monthly summaries, and keeping your account secure. We do not sell your personal data or use it for targeted advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and are only accessible to you through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may edit, export, or delete your data at any time from within the app.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party services for functions such as hosting, fonts, or analytics. These providers only process the minimum data necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on the Spendly website. We're happy to help.
